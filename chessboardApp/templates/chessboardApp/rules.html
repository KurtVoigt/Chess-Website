CINS 465: Assignment 4 by Kurt Voigtritter

The goal of Chess is to kill the opponent's King piece before they kill yours using soldiers that move in unique ways around the grid board. There are 6 types of pieces in varying amounts:

  • Pawn: can move forward 2 spaces on first move, one space forward from then on, attacks diagonally
  • Knight: Can move one step vertically and two horizontally, or two steps vertically and one horizontally, any square it lands on is an attack
  • Bishop: Can move any number of spaces diagonally
  • Rook: Can move any number of spaces vertically and horizontally
  • Queen: Combines the power of the rook and the bishop, can move any number of spaces vertically, horizontally, or diagonally, but only one direction per turn
    • May move one square in any direction
